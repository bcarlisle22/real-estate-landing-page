Carlisle Real Estate was founded in 2002 by John Carlisle, a licensed real estate broker with over 20 years of experience in the industry.
Starting out as a small, family-owned business, Carlisle Real Estate has since grown into a leading real estate agency in the region, with a team of experienced agents and staff members who are committed to providing top-quality service to clients. At Carlisle Real Estate, our mission is to provide the highest level of service to our clients by utilizing our expertise, knowledge, and resources to help them achieve their real estate goals. We strive to build lasting relationships with our clients based on trust, integrity, and mutual respect. We are dedicated to delivering outstanding results through hard work, dedication, and attention to detail.

Our team of experienced agents and staff members are committed to providing personalized service to each of our clients, whether they are buying or selling a home, renting a property, or seeking property management services. We understand that the real estate market can be complex and daunting, and we are here to guide our clients through every step of the process with honesty, transparency, and professionalism. In short, our goal at Carlisle Real Estate is to make the real estate experience as seamless and stress-free as possible for our clients, while providing them with the best possible results.
